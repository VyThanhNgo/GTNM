Image Sizes

Banner Image: 1200x400px

This image is typically used for the header or main visual area of the website. It should be large and high-quality to capture attention.

Icon Image: 100x100px

Icons are small images used for branding or representing features. They should be simple and recognizable even at a smaller size.

Logo Image: 300x100px

The logo image should be clear and crisp, typically used for branding purposes on the website header or footer.

Thumbnail Image: 150x150px

Thumbnails are smaller versions of larger images used for previews in galleries, blog posts, or content listings.
